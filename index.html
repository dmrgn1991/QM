Player's Guide

QuestManager is a fantasy management clicker game. The object is to use a hired team of heroes to delve the dungeon. The dungeon is a dangerous place filled with insidious traps, vicious monsters, and piles of ancient treasure...

The gameplay loop consists of a lot of clicking. Hire some fantasy heroes, send them into the depths, click until they've completed their quest and returned to town. From there, you can use the interface to handle upkeep such as repairing or upgrading equipment and healing your heroes.

Player Stats

Heroes have four important statistics.

  • HP - The Hero's health points, rises with level
  • Combat Rating - The Hero's ability to fight, raise by upgrading equipment
  • Skill Rating - The Hero's capability in disarming traps, rises with level
  • Magic Rating - Applies to both combat and traps, rises with level or by finding magic items

Afflictions

Heroes can be cursed, denoted by an * asterisk next to their name. Curses can lower Combat Rating, Skill Rating or HP. Dispel the curse at the chapel.

Objective

The objective of the game is to see how many levels your Heroes can acquire before all of them die in a Stygian pit and you run out of money. Fun!
